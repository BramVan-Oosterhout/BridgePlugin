---++ Features
The BridgeApp is a wiki application to collect and display bridge hands stored in Foswiki data topics. The rendering of the displays is supported by the Bridge plugin. Navigation and  presentation details are handled by the application.

A new web is created for each deployment of the application. Data entry is supported on the WebHome page and entered hands are displayed with their major characteristics.
